About

Richard "Lennon" Audrain, PhD is an enrolled citizen of the Shawnee Tribe and Cherokee Nation. He serves as Head of Innovation and Policy Initiatives for the Next Education Workforce Initiative at Arizona State University's Mary Lou Fulton College for Teaching and Learning Innovation, where he also holds the position of Research Assistant Professor in the Division for Advancing Educator Preparation.

His research and practice focus on transforming how educational systems recruit, prepare, and organize educators. Through strategic policy design and innovative workforce models, his work addresses the evolving needs of schools and communities in an increasingly complex educational landscape.
